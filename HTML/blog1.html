Exercise tips for women suffering from PCOS

  • 1st Apr 2016

PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome) is a lifestyle caused endemic rising alarmingly in women in India. Losing weight is one of the most recommended natural remedy suggested by doctors to ease some of the symptoms (cysts on ovaries, facial hair, infertility and weight gain). Diet control and keeping yourself fit through exercise are your best bet against PCOS. In this article, we will explore some of the most common exercise tips which will help you manage this condition better.

Cardio vs. Strength Training

A mix of both cardio and strength training is necessary to drive the different benefits from these types of exercises. Cardio helps to raise your heart rate and thereby, increase calories burnt leading to weight loss. Strength training helps build muscle to increase your basal metabolic rate.

Duration

While there's no hard and fast rule around duration of sessions, most studies suggest approx. 30 minutes of exercise about 5 times a week. An ideal mix would be 3 cardio sessions and 2 sessions dedicated to strength training. However, it's better to start slow (in case you aren't in habit of exercising) and build stamina to reach your comfort level. Always listen to your body and stop in case you are unable to continue.

Type of exercise

While there are numerous options available, some of the most common and fun activities are listed here.

Running
Zumba
Yoga
Swimming
Cycling
Aerobic Classes
Pilates

Try and find an exercise partner so that you both can motivate each other and keep continuity.
