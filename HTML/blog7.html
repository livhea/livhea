Extend your run...

  • 22nd Apr 2016

Running is a passion and more and more people are taking it up these days to keep themselves fit. However, running long distances on a regular basis is not an easy task and many folks end up giving up on running as they lose motivation / interest after a while. Below listed are few simple interventions which can make your long runs better and more enjoyable:

• Rest up at least a day before your run
• Be aware of weather and its impact on your running efficiency / speed
• Carry with yourself the right sets of fluids, dry fruits etc. (as per what works best for your body)
• Use Vaseline and similar products to reduce friction from chafing
• Use the opportunity to explore new places (Outdoor running is better than running on a treadmill)
• Make running buddies even if it’s for short distance
• Post run, bring your heart rate down gradually by cooling down
• Eat / drink within 30 minutes to make up for some of the lost calories
