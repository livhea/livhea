Swim towards weight loss

  • 18th Apr 2016

Summers are a great time for all swimming lovers (not enough temperature controlled swimming pools for winters in India – sigh!). Not only it’s a great exercise, but a welcome relief from the hot and sultry weather that generally prevails over majority of the country. However, its impact as an exercise for weight loss are not clear in the minds of all swimming enthusiasts.

Swimming is like other low level cardio exercises such as walking. Like all cardio exercises, when you start swimming at the beginning of a season, you tend to rapidly lose weight. However, as your body becomes accustom to the new exercise regime, the propensity to lose weight starts going down significantly. In such a scenario, while maintaining your love for the pool, the best way to lose weight combines the following practices:

• Complement with other workouts such as yoga, weights or even running
• Mix up your speed by doing fast and slow laps instead of always swimming at similar pace
• Diet control by eating protein rich food and restricting intake of products with artificial sugar
